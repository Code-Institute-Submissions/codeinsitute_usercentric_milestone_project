What is PaddyOS?

PaddyOS is a robust and reliable operative system based on Linux for everyone.

Easy to Use, Bang on!

No-nonsense and easy to learn UI with everything right where you need. We don't use "bloatware" or other nuisances.

Based on Linux

From smartphones to cars, supercomputers and home appliances, home desktops to enterprise servers, the Linux operating system is everywhere.

Besides being the platform of choice to run desktops, servers, and embedded systems across the globe, Linux is one of the most reliable, secure and worry-free operating systems available.More info

Free & Free! No costs, total control!

PaddyOS is free to install, as we beleive that everyone should have access to a reliable system without costs and control. The Computer is yours, why should you pay or be tracked by adds?
